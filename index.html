Mushroom picking with Funguys!

Gobar is an app to help you identify and keep track of found mushrooms.

Get Started

Especially popular among Slovenians, you will not want to miss this!

Introducing Gobar, the ultimate mushroom picking app for all nature enthusiasts. With Gobar, you can easily identify mushrooms with a snap of your phone's camera. No more confusion about which mushrooms are edible and which are not - Gobar takes care of that for you!

But that's not all. Gobar also allows you to save the location of your mushroom finds on a map, making it easy to revisit your favorite spots. You can even share your finds with other users, creating a community of mushroom hunters.

And for those who want to keep track of their mushroom discoveries, Gobar includes a mushroom library. With locked mushrooms waiting to be discovered and unlocked, you'll always have a reason to keep exploring.

So what are you waiting for? Download Gobar today and start exploring the world of mushrooms!

Benefits

Check out some of the benefits Gobar offers

Modern technology

Gobar leverages the power of cutting-edge technology to accurately identify mushrooms, utilizing an API that employs pre-trained convolutional neural networks. Additionally, the app incorporates best practices for Android development, ensuring a seamless user experience.

Potential

Gobar has the potential to expand beyond mushrooms, featuring other edible plants with medicinal and cooking applications. Users can explore nature and learn about these plants in an engaging way with unexpected discoveries.

Entertainment

Using Gobar is not only informative but also entertaining as users explore and discover new mushrooms. And for those lucky enough to stumble upon psychedelic mushrooms, Gobar offers a fun surprise. The app provides an exciting way to engage with nature and learn about the world of mushrooms.

Sustainability

Gobar also promotes sustainability by encouraging people to forage for their own food. By finding and consuming wild mushrooms, users can reduce their reliance on store-bought, imported food, thus minimizing their carbon footprint. With Gobar, not only can you explore the natural world, but you can also contribute to a more sustainable future.
